East Coast Park

Looking for an outdoor adventure or a place to unwind after a tiring week?
Look no further because East Coast Park is the right place to be!

Activities ECP has to offer:

Kayaking/ Picnic/ Cycling/ Fishing/ Playground/ Water Ski

Beyond the Shores

Paddle away in a kayak with your friends and family.
Click here to find out more on the rates.

Keep Calm and and Have a Picnic

Looking for a retreat from work or school?
Time to pack your picnic baskets and be ready to have some fun in the sand and sun!

Pedal for Fun

Rent a bicycle and have a leisurely ride along the East Coast Park Connector.
In just 30 minutes, you'd be able to enjoy the scenery of Singapore's beautiful skyline.

Let's Go Fishing

Choose a comfortable spot on the Bedok Jetty and fish away...
Enjoy the sea breeze and with any luck, you might be going home with some dinner!

Bring out the kid in you!

Have some fun at the Marine Cove playground! So many unique and interesting facilities that are sure to keep your children occupied (possibly for hours)!

Time to make some waves

Looking for a new sport to try out? Singapore Wake Park @ ECP is the place for you to try water ski for the first time! Click here for more info!
