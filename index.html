East Coast Park

Looking for an outdoor adventure or a place to unwind after a tiring week?
Look no further because East Coast Park is the right place to be!

Here are some activities ECP has to offer:

Kayaking/ Picnic/ Cycling/ Fishing/ Playground/ Water Ski

Beyond the Shores

Brave the heat and start paddling away in a kayak with your friends and family.
For more information and rates, click here!

Keep Calm and Have a Picnic

Looking for a retreat to escape from the stress of school or work?
Time to pack your picnic baskets and be ready to have some fun in the sand and sun!

Pedal for Fun

Rent a bicycle from the available rental shops and have a leisurely ride along the East Coast Park Connector. In just 30 minutes, you'd be able to enjoy the scenery of Singapore's beautiful skyline.

Let's Go Fishing

Be the first to 'chope' a comfortable spot on the Bedok Jetty and start fishing away...
Enjoy the calming sea breeze and with any luck, you might just be going home with some yummy seafood!

Bring out the kid in you!

Have some fun at the Marine Cove playground! So many unique and interesting facilities that are sure to keep your children occupied (possibly for hours)!

Time to make some waves

Ever wanted to try a new water sport but not sure where? Singapore Wake Park @ ECP is the just the place for you! Here's your chance to try water skiing for the first time!
